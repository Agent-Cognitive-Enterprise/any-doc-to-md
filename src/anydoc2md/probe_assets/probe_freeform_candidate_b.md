# Warehouse Handover Packet

This packet is used when a tote moves from the receiving dock to the overnight shelf.

## Transfer steps
1. Scan the dock marker.
2. Seal the amber tote.
3. Attach the handover card.
4. Record the freezer reading.
5. Sign the turnover ledger.
6. Store the tote in bay C.
